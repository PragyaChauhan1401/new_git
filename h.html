Chandrayaan-3: India's Lunar Exploration Mission

  • Mission Type: Lunar Exploration
  • Agency: Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO)
  • Mission Status: In Development
  • Objective:To further explore the Moon's surface.

    • Mission Details

  • Chandrayaan-3 is the third lunar exploration mission by ISRO, building upon the successes of Chandrayaan-1 and Chandrayaan-2.
  • The mission aims to send a lander and rover to the Moon's surface to conduct scientific experiments, gather data, and capture images. It consists of a lander named Vikram and a rover named Pragyan, similar to those of the Chandrayaan-2 mission. The propulsion module carried the lander and rover configuration to lunar orbit in preparation for a powered descent by the lander.

    • Current Status
